What is the
Transcaucasian Trail?

We are building a world-class, long-distance hiking trail - the Transcaucasian Trail (TCT) - across the Caucasus, linking roughly two dozen existing and proposed national parks in the region.

The Caucasus, on the border of Europe and Asia, is one of the most biologically and culturally diverse mountain ranges in the world, but one that few have had the chance to explore. Now our growing community of hikers, cartographers and conservationists is putting this hidden gem on the map to help ensure that the natural and cultural heritage of the region can be enjoyed by future generations.

We will make hiking in the Caucasus safer and more accessible for everyone while increasing economic opportunities for local communities and helping protect the environment. We are starting to build the TCT in Georgia this summer. Join us to be part of this incredible project in one of the most intriguing regions on Earth.

Get Involved
