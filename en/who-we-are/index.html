The Transcaucasian Trail Community

Promoting Development of the Trail

We are a growing community of interested hikers, mappers, conservationists and supporters who want to improve the hiking opportunities in the Caucasus for locals and tourists alike. So far we have mapped hundreds kilometers of trail that will become a part of a long-distance hiking network. As the trail is mapped, we will be producing hiking guides for the region and planning trail improvement activities.

You can join us by clicking on the button below to sign up for updates on our activities and future volunteer opportunities. Also be sure to find us on Facebook and Instagram.
