Goals

In 2016, we focused on building the first section of the TCT in Svaneti, in the north-west of Georgia. We also scouted the Lesser Caucasus in southern Georgia and in Armenia. In Svaneti, we ran volunteer camps and worked on building new and improving the existing infrastructure along this route, mapping and marking the trails, highlighting the cultural sites as well as engaging with local communities to build their support for the project. Click here to read more about what we did in 2016.

The goals for 2017 are:

  • The Svaneti section of the TCT is now open to hike. We will produce a basic guide to the TCT in Svaneti (GPS routes, with basic trail descriptions) and make it available on our website before the 2017 hiking season starts (late Spring).
  • We will expand our volunteer programme and run more camps this summer, in both Georgia and Armenia. We will be posting updates on that soon through this website and on the TCT social media.
  • We will keep consulting the local communities along the trail, and engaging them in trail development. We plan to engage a larger group of local stakeholders and volunteers, and start building networks and knowledge in Azerbaijan and Abkhazia.
